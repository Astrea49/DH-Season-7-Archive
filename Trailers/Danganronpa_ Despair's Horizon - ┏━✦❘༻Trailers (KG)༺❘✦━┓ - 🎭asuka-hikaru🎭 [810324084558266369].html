Asuka Hikaru BOT 12-Mar-21 03:41 AM
- Where does it hurt? -
An illuminated trailer in the middle of the night stands out as the only apparent trailer with signs of activity tonight. Everyone else is either away, or sleeping in the darkness and comfort of their bed. Not Asuka. She's scared of the dark. Scared to the point that she has panic attacks if there isn't some sort of light source to help her see the things around her. The complete and total helpless feeling of being surrounded by the void is something she is better off without. T̶̍͑h̸͖̀e̸̢͋ ̷͑̏d̷̋̕ä̷̞́r̵͔̀k̵͑̔ń̶̬ȇ̷͉s̷͐̏s̶̻͛ ̷̩̐i̵̽͒s̵̀͋ ̷̔̅d̶̾̄e̴͂͂a̴͌͒d̸̄̈́ļ̶̏y̴̔͠ It's within this trailer where Asuka feels most comfortable. Wrapped up in the warm blankets, overlooked by angelic lights, surrounded by the cosplays she has made over the course of her life here. It's a much more desirable place to be than that cold, dark, and uncomfortable tree she slept in for a few nights. She didn't let her fear be known because a personality like her has to remain consistent. No weakness other than the ones already shown to the others. Revealing more will only add to the risk of existing. As observed before, there are definitely people capable of killing. In fact, we just had a murder not too long ago. It's a scary, albeit true prospect. Asuka stretches out her arms and proceeds to get up. Assuming there's a mirror in here, she faces it. Otherwise, she looks at a wall. Regardless, she places her right hand in her hair, and...pulls it off... Apparently Asuka's pink hair is just a wig. Why would she try to hide her true hair? Her short, black hair that fits better with her heterochromatic eyes? Isn't she a cosplayer? Dying her hair wouldn't make sense if she could just wear wigs and colour those instead. "Asuka... You need to actually think... Complacency right now? That's not what Sora or Haruhi would accept. You've always been the little sibling of the group. You have to grow..."
With the wig in her hands, Asuka starts shaking a little bit. It's as if she were anxious about something...
[FLASHBACK: HIGHSCHOOL FESTIVAL]
"Is that what you truly want to do?" "I'm...not sure... They said it would be cool, and I don't want to let them all down." "Only conform to them if you're willing to give yourself up. Otherwise you're a pushover." "..." It's the second day of Asuka's highschool culture festival. She has just ran into an older girl. One wearing a lab coat. Probably a doctor that had a younger sibling or something. That's all she can think of. Right now, she's shaking out of sheer anxiety. She knows she looks good in a maid outfit, and so does everyone else. So why does she doubt herself? It's because up until she became an ultimate, Asuka never really had much confidence. Even nowadays, it can be seen sometimes. The cracks in her personality are clear and exploitable given enough time and exposure. Back to the matter at hand. The older girl hands Asuka what appears to be a Japanese lucky charm. It's a common item around, so she's not surprised that someone quite a bit older than her has possession of one. Not that the older girl believes in luck anyway... "Hey...what's your name?" "Hmm? Just call me Rei. I doubt we may ever meet again, but if our paths happen to cross again, you'll know." "Alright Rei, thank you." With that, Asuka heads back to her classroom. Her class is doing a cosplay cafe, and Asuka is the head waitress for obvious reasons. The day is about to begin, and the demand for food will be high. They'll have to put together an all-time performance in order to come out on top of the rest of the classes. It's been war since the day they all stepped into their classrooms.
Break
"Rei...I remember you from back then. You know I still have your lucky charm. I wonder if I'll ever be able to return it to you again..." Asuka sits down on her bed, and takes a deep sigh.
Resume
Asuka's flashback fast forwards into the future. She's...in her room now. It's a vivid picture. There's smoke in the room, a small fire that came from elsewhere has started in her room now. Her door flies open. It's Sora. He picks up Asuka who at that point is half conscious but still responsive enough to tell what's going on. She's able to tell her twin brother what happened, before promptly blacking out. After what felt like months, which was only actually a day or so, Asuka woke up feeling completely fine. The only problem was that the smoke and fire left her with permanent lung damage. That's something she'll never share with another person because she doesn't want to have to trouble them with her own conditions. It's not something that should ever be brought up anyways considering they fact that it'll most likely remain an irrelevant secret.
- THROW ME TO THE FLAMES -
*Asuka was continuously plagued by nightmares which included fires. A lot of them quite deadly, and the ones where she survived were often the ones that she would actually suffer from the most. This would be a common theme for about a month. She would requently awake from nightmares of being set ablaze. Whether by accident, or for some reason...intentional. Asuka's mind during that time frame was a lot more wild, meaning that the more extreme things one could think of felt a lot more realistic to her than it would today.
Pause
"...I think I should count myself lucky that I'm not afraid of fire after what happened to me. I'm luck to have gotten away with one of the more favourable outcomes... If things were worse... I may have not been able to become the Ultimate Cosplay Creator." A smile creeps on her face as she thinks of that. Of course, thinking of the positives will always work in one's favour. Although it has not yet subsided, as every now and then, she has a dream about the flames...
- Watch me burn -
Asuka then remembers something important. The reason she's a lot more motivated than someone in her position would be. It's the saddest part of her whole story, and especially one of the most important parts. Death of a loved one. "...Haruhi..."
Resume
How many years ago was it now. Asuka's mind surrounding that has become so hazy that she has trouble even thinking of it. Yes she's scared to death of the dark, but this as well is quite unnerving. If it wasn't to anyone in her position, they could be considered heartless. Regardless, both Asuka, and her twin brother Sora, did not take the news very well. Haruhi Hikaru, dead. "WHAT DO YOU MEAN HARU-NEE IS DEAD?!??" "Asuka...it was...it was shown to us. She was killed..." It was at this point in life that Asuka had come into her own as the Ultimate Cosplay Creator. She had all sorts of commissions and whatnot, but had to put it all on hold because the most important figure in her life was taken from her in an instant. She was out of it for a good two weeks, slowly reclaiming her grasp on reality. The reality that her older sister would no longer be there with her and her brother. Her brother was acting all tough so Asuka could follow suit, but that didn't pan out and the two remained rather quiet by their standards until they were able to move on after a very, very tough time.
[FLASHBACK END]
Still sitting on the bed, tears are rolling down her eyes. The memories of her older sister are still quite painful indeed, but it's thanks to her than Asuka is able to keep moving. "The world keeps going. Theoretically, so can we, so keep going. Use the past as a reference when moving forward. Learn." A lot nicer than what Rei would say let's be real "Even now, you're still the smartest person I know... No matter what, you remain spicy..." A laugh comes out of her again as she stands back up. Remember her hair, yeah, she undoes the little bob it was in, revealing...
Yeah, that's what Asuka looks like without her pink wig on. She normally ties her hair together or just cuts it outright in order to get a good fit with her wigs. Right now she's opting to just tie it up because it's simper and she doesn't run the risk of cutting herself accidentally. And oh boy has she cut herself many times over when trying to trim her hair. A cosplay creator still has to see in order to work, and considering that she can't, it would of course result in bodily harm. Recently though, she has gotten quite a bit better, but she's not the ultimate hair dresser. No perfection here. Moving on, Asuka then remembers what she's doing. A while back, Asuka met with Tori, and then Eanna. The latter needed some replacement clothes. Asuka found it strange that she didn't just grab a spare pair conveniently located in her own trailer, but she isn't gonna complain when she can make her own clothes for someone.
[Cosplay Creation: Begin]
With her power of cosplay creation, Asuka has the measurements and colour. She also took a mental image of Eanna's clothes to replicate the look of it. Remembering Eanna getting all flustered when she mentioned all that stuff is still rather fresh in her head. She found it quite funny, and didn't pay it much mind herself. Though there are always implications to her words even if she doesn't mean it that way. Well, Asuka doesn't really care. She'll make a move on whoever if they interest her enough. Using Eanna's current clothes as a reference, Asuka starts working. Her invaluable sewing kit is the reason why she's able to even do this. That and the premade cosplays, and the costumes she borrowed and not stole from the costume room a bit back. Replacing the look with the same materials is a rather simple task. One that you could ask of anyone half decent at what they're doing. That doesn't matter. Eanna came to the best, so she'll get the best. This is going to be...different however...
"She'll love this." Seeing as Eanna's clothes were primarily warm and bright colours, some cooler colours would work very nicely. A nice mix of black, grey, and dark blue will do the trick. Add in a bit of white to add some depth to the look and it'll look fantastic. The cape, made of similar stuff to Eanna's original cape, will be coloured dark blue. The main body will be black with white splashes her and there. Just a little bit of an Asuka touch. The grey will fade in from the black as the clothing extends outwards. So it's black and white closer to the center, and in places such as the sleeves will be grey with a little less white. It's kind of like some sort of colour fusion, and that's why Asuka is making it that way. Aesthetically speaking, she likes it just like that. Fast forward around half an hour, and Asuka is done with her first pair of it. She'll make one more just for the hell of it becuase people need some variety in their lives. Asuka doesn't even wear the same thing twice nowadays. There are a ton of costumes to choose from. She'll realistically never run out of options while she's here. Maybe she should steal those instead of trying to take a giant killer robot... And then...after more work...Asuka finishes. Two pairs of clothing for the person who requested it from her. Asking people to pay for these sort of things would not make things better whatsoever. They're at risk of death, Asuka feels that something like that is already bad enough. Her prices are normally kinda high anyway thanks to her title...
[Bonus Round]
Asuka does have energy for one more thing before she goes to sleep. A secret project that has been in the works for a while. She has to get the wig and the clothing absolutely spot on before she can show it to everyone else. In her eyes, this will be her best work. This is mainly due to how long she's taking, and how careful she is. "They'll all be in awe after this..."
