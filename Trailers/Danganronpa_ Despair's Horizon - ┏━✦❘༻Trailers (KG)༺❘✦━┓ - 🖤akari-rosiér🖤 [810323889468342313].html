Her curiosity always got the better of her, and so she read it through blurry eyes. Eanna… Could she be… worried about her? The thought made her warm up a bit. It was easy to forget in her current state, but if she were to be honest, she was already starting to come around to appreciating her presence. At least, the thought that someone like her cared for her was calming. Like a small light in the never-ending darkness… She didn’t want to bother her too much… but, if she didn’t mind spending time together, Akari could use a bit of comfort. She usually preferred being alone, but it was starting to reach a dangerous point.
